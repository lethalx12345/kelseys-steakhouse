About Kelsey's

A local favorite for steak, craft sides, and straightforward hospitality.

The Story

Kelsey's Steak House is a popular, family-owned restaurant known for open charcoal grilling and hand-cut USDA beef. The menu centers on steakhouse classics with a focus on quality cuts, prime rib, and sides made to match.

What to Expect

Whether it's a date night or a family dinner, the goal is the same: a reliable meal with solid portions, attentive service, and a menu that doesn't overcomplicate a great steak.

Standouts

Prime rib is a house staple, and the kitchen builds around open-flame cooking. USDA beef is hand-cut, and the menu is sized for guests who want a full steakhouse experience without unnecessary extras.

Menu Highlights

A focused steakhouse menu built around beef, sides, and a few reliable favorites.
